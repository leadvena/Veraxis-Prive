# Design System

## Colors
- **Primary**: #1C1917 (Rich Stone)
- **Secondary**: #44403C (Warm Stone)
- **CTA**: #CA8A04 (Gold Accent)
- **Background**: #FAFAF9 (Stone White)
- **Text**: #0C0A09 (Deep Stone)

## Typography
- **Heading**: Playfair Display (Elegant, Serif)
- **Body**: Inter (Clean, Modern, Sans-serif)

## Spacing & Layout
- Fluid transitions and morphing elements.
- Horizontal scroll journey for service discovery.
- Backdrop blurs and liquid glass effects.

## Motion
- Smooth transitions (400-600ms).
- Morphing SVG/CSS elements.
